One to One Readings

One to one readings are available to book for yourself or as a gift for someone else.

Telephone Readings:-

Telephone readings are available. Trinity does not need to be with you in person to communicate with your loved ones and deliver their messages. Readings cost £25 per person. To book a date please click here.The team will contact you to confirm a date and send you a link for payment.

Group bookings for One to One Readings:-

Trinity will visit in person for group bookings of 5 or more. Readings cost £30 per person plus a travel cost. To book a date please click here. The team will contact you to confirm cost, a date and send you a link for payment.

Visit Trinity for a One to One Reading:-

You can visit Trinity in person for a one to one reading. Readings cost £25 per person. To book a date please click here. The team will contact you to confirm a date and send you a link for payment.
